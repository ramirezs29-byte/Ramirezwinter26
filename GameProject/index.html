Introduction

Softball is a sport where two teams take turns hitting a ball and running bases to score points.

Why You Should Play

It's fun, helps you stay active, and teaches teamwork.

Summary

Softball is a sport where you gain knowledge and build teamwork skills.

My Personal Experience

I have been playing the sport for six years. I first started when I was five in T-ball. As I grew older, at age eight I played baseball (with the boys) for two years because there were few girls. I enjoyed the game and decided to start playing softball, eventually moving to a slightly competitive level called travel ball, where we sometimes play against other teams to compete for a ring or a trophy in tournaments.
