Instructions On How To Play

Softball is a seven-inning game played by two teams. The objective is to score as many runs as possible by hitting the ball.

Key Terms And Rules Explained

Softball teams usually have nine players, though rosters can be larger. The pitcher throws the ball to the batter, who tries to hit it. A strike is a good pitch that the batter misses or does not swing at. A ball is a pitch outside the strike zone. An inning is when each team gets a turn to bat and field.

My Personal Experience

I have been playing the sport for six years. I first started when I was five in T-ball. As I grew older, at age eight I played baseball (with the boys) for two years because there were few girls. I enjoyed the game and decided to start playing softball, eventually moving to a slightly competitive level called travel ball, where we sometimes play against other teams to compete for a ring or a trophy in tournaments.
