Interesting Dolphin Facts

  • Dolphins are very intelligent
  • Dolphins live in oceans around the world
  • Dolphins communicate using a variety of sounds
  • Dolphins are social animals and live in groups called pods
  • Dolphins can swim at speeds of up to 25 miles per hour
