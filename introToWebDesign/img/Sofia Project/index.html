My Favorite City: Sofia, Bulgaria

About Sofia

Sofia is the capital and largest city of Bulgaria, located in the western part of the country. It is known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and stunning architecture.

Sofia has a population of around 1.2 million people and is a major economic and cultural hub in the region. The city is home to numerous museums, galleries, theaters, and music venues, making it a vibrant cultural center.

One of the most iconic landmarks in Sofia is the Alexander Nevsky Cathedral, a stunning Orthodox cathedral that dominates the city skyline. Other notable attractions include the National Palace of Culture, the Boyana Church, and the Vitosha Mountain, which offers breathtaking views of the city.

Why I Love Sofia

I love Sofia for its unique blend of history and modernity. The city has a rich cultural heritage, with ancient ruins and historic buildings coexisting alongside modern architecture and vibrant nightlife. Sofia is also known for its warm and welcoming people, who are proud of their city's heritage and culture.
