Chicken Stir Fry

Chinese Chicken Dish

This is a classic Chinese chicken dish made with chicken, vegetables, and a savory sauce.


Indredients:

  • 4 cups water
  • 2 cups white rice
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2/3 cup low-sodium soy sauce,or to taste
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • ¼ teaspoon red chili flakes
  • 3 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil, divided
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 head broccoli, broken into florets
  • 1 onion, cut into large chunks
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • 1 (8 ounce) can sliced water chestnuts, drained
  • 1 green bell pepper, cut into matchsticks

How to Make It:

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Bring water to a boil in a medium saucepan. Stir in rice, reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer until rice is tender and water is absorbed, about 20 minutes.
  3. Whisk soy sauce, brown sugar, cornstarch, ginger, garlic, and red chili flakes together in a bowl until smooth.
  4. Heat 1 tablespoon sesame oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add chicken slices and cook until no longer pink in the center, about 5 minutes. Remove chicken from skillet and set aside.
  5. Add remaining 1 tablespoon sesame oil to the skillet. Stir in red bell pepper, broccoli, onion, carrots, water chestnuts, and green bell pepper. Cook and stir until vegetables are tender-crisp, about 5 minutes.
  6. Return chicken to the skillet. Pour soy sauce mixture over chicken and vegetables. Cook and stir until sauce has thickened and everything is heated through, about 2 minutes.
  7. Serve chicken stir fry over cooked rice. Enjoy Your Meal!
